# Lyzr Platform Capabilities Reference

## Platform Identity
Lyzr AI is an Enterprise Agent Production Infrastructure offering autonomous AI agents for business operations.

## Core Platform Features

### Knowledge Graph as a Service
Intelligent reasoning over complex entity relationships — contracts, suppliers, org charts, product catalogs. Enables agents to understand connections between data, not just retrieve documents.

### Hallucination Manager
Ensures accurate, grounded responses. Critical for regulated industries (banking, insurance, healthcare) and customer-facing agents where factual accuracy is non-negotiable.

### Orchestration
Run multiple models and tools as one system. Enables multi-agent workflows where specialized agents collaborate on complex tasks.

### Knowledge Base
Connect organizational data to agent context. Supports documents, URLs, databases, and APIs as knowledge sources with RAG and vector search.

### Responsible AI
Built-in compliance, safety auditing, and governance. Audit trails, bias detection, human oversight mechanisms.

## Products

### LyzrGPT
Enterprise-grade secure GPT for internal operations. Private, compliant AI access for employees.

### Architect
No-code agent builder — "Build your agentic system with just a prompt." Visual builder that accepts structured build plans to scaffold agent applications.

### Agents as a Service
Pre-integrated, ready-to-deploy agent solutions for common business functions.

## Fit Scoring Criteria

### Strong Fit Indicators (score high)
- High-volume customer support operations
- Multi-channel communication
- Complex procurement / vendor management
- Regulated industry (banking, insurance, healthcare)
- Large workforce (HR/L&D needs)
- B2B sales with long cycles
- Document-heavy workflows
- Existing CRM/ERP systems to integrate with

### Moderate Fit Indicators
- Growing team with scaling challenges
- Manual processes but low volume
- Some automation already in place
- Tech-forward culture but early AI adoption

### Low Fit Indicators
- Very small team (< 10 people)
- Simple product with minimal support needs
- No document-heavy workflows
- Consumer-only with no B2B component
