# Lyzr Competitor Benchmarker

## Purpose
Analyze a company's competitors to show where AI-powered Lyzr agents create differentiation and competitive advantage. Highlight what competitors are doing with AI and where the target company can leapfrog them using Lyzr.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Identify Competitors
Using the target company's website and industry:
- Identify 3–5 direct competitors
- Note their AI/automation adoption signals (chatbots, AI features, automation mentions)

### Step 2: AI Adoption Benchmark

For each competitor, assess:

| Signal | Check For |
|--------|-----------|
| Customer Support AI | Chatbots, virtual assistants, help center AI |
| Sales Automation | AI SDR tools, personalization, lead scoring |
| Document AI | Automated processing, extraction, OCR |
| Knowledge Management | AI search, knowledge bases, RAG indicators |
| Internal Automation | Process automation mentions, efficiency claims |
| Responsible AI | Privacy policies, AI ethics statements, compliance claims |

Rate each competitor's AI maturity: **None / Basic / Moderate / Advanced**

### Step 3: Gap Analysis

For each department where the target company scores well on Lyzr fit:
- What are competitors doing in this area?
- Where is the target company behind?
- Where can they leapfrog with Lyzr?
- What's the cost of inaction (competitor advantage grows)?

### Step 4: Competitive Advantage Map

Show how each recommended Lyzr agent creates competitive advantage:

```
| Lyzr Agent | Competitor Status | Your Advantage |
|------------|------------------|----------------|
| AI Support Agent | [Competitor X] has basic chatbot | Full AI resolution with Knowledge Graph — 3x better deflection |
| AI SDR | No competitor uses AI SDR | First-mover advantage in outbound |
| Contract Review | [Competitor Y] manual review (5 days) | AI review in hours with Hallucination Manager |
```

### Step 5: Market Positioning Statement

Generate a 2–3 sentence positioning statement:
"By deploying [Lyzr agents], [Company] can [competitive advantage] while competitors still [current competitor approach]. This positions [Company] as the AI-first leader in [industry/segment]."
